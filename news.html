What'up in Mango Central ?

  • The first Mango Outpost have opened in Annecy, France!

    After months of planning, we are very happy to finally open our first physical shop in Annecy, where Mango Central was originally created. There you can buy mangoes, mango milkshake but also goodies. Feel free to say hi!

  • Banana Middle is out of the market

    The famous Chinese banana-selling company is no longer a threat to us. By gathering the evidences found on the Mango Outposts construction sites, our diplomacy team was able to convince Banana Middle to give up on their initial plans. They will not interfere with us, and we won't interfere with them.

    December 2023

  • Banana peel found in a Mango Outpost construction site

    The walls have ears these days. It is more and more obvious that our construction sites are being spied on by no other than Banana Middle, our major rival on the fruit market. We won't let this happen, our best detectives are currently investigating and we will keep you in touch.

  • Our Indian branch just opened !

    Today is a great day for mango people : we just opened our first subsidiary in the world's first mango producer : India! This expansion will allow us to not only sell mangoes but also produce them directly under the perfect weather. Since this is all pretty new, there is a lot of job offers there, and anyone willing to go abroad is welcome! A conference about good practice in Indian workspace will soon be held in our office for those interested.
